About Club

Our club are for all students who want to improve their English, teamworking, brainstrom and sloving skills. We created a lot type of club like Cooking clubs, Board game clubs, Mixed words club, Story clubs and more. In special events, we will create special events clubs such as Ester Eggs Club, Valentine Club, Christmas Club and many more. The important thing is that every club has many prizes for the winning team and participants.
